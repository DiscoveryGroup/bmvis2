Operations on nodes and edges

  • Double-click nodes to zoom their neighborhood to window
  • Click to select nodes
  • Hold down Control for multiple selection
  • Drag nodes to re-position them
  • Right-click (or Ctrl-click on Mac) to open context menu (e.g. to view node in browser)
  • Hold down Shift for rectangle selection

Navigation

  • Drag on the white background to pan around
  • Double-click on the white background to fit view to window
  • The scroll wheel can be used to zoom
